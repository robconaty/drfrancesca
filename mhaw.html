In The Looking Glass

    Photo credit: João Jesus, Pexels

    By Sarah L.

    Before, there was darkness in the mirror,

    A deep dark vortex of sadness and fear,

    Endless days of trudging, running and hiding,

    No chance of the beauty in life me finding.


    Now looking in the mirror I understand that I am not the darkness,

    With help and compassion I can also see lightness.


    A lightness I never thought I would ever see.

    Joy seeps in and times of happiness slowly come to be.


    Yes there are also times of sadness and fear and anger,

    But in the looking glass I am beginning to see and be a kind, compassionate me.


    I no longer avoid the looking glass nor want to escape through it,

    Learning to nurture the past, the present and future me.
